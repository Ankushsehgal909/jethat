JetHat Cyber Security Pvt. Ltd.

Securing the Future of Digital Innovation

JetHat Cyber Security Pvt. Ltd. is a forward-focused technology company based in Noida, India, founded with a singular vision: to redefine how organizations engage with digital transformation by making security, scalability, and innovation the pillars of growth.

Our Mission & Vision

In an era where cyber threats grow more sophisticated by the day and businesses increasingly rely on digital infrastructure, JetHat serves as a trusted partner, guiding companies through complexity with clarity, confidence, and cutting-edge solutions.

Our core specialization lies in cybersecurity, cloud infrastructure, and AI-driven software development—a powerful trio of competencies that allow us to build future-ready digital ecosystems for businesses across industries. We don't just plug vulnerabilities—we design systems that anticipate and adapt to them.

Whether you're a growing startup, an established enterprise, or a public institution, JetHat offers a strategic technology backbone that enables you to innovate without compromise.

Cybersecurity Excellence

Our cybersecurity services are engineered to offer more than just protection—they provide peace of mind. Through advanced threat detection and prevention, real-time monitoring, secure code reviews, and comprehensive vulnerability assessment and penetration testing (VAPT), we help clients create resilient environments.

We also believe in empowering people, which is why we offer security awareness training programs that turn employees into your first line of defense.

Full-Spectrum Development

Beyond security, JetHat is a full-spectrum technology solutions provider, offering a wide array of custom software development services. From full-stack web platforms and cross-platform mobile apps to intuitive, responsive user interfaces, we build digital experiences that are as powerful as they are seamless.

Our cloud solutions are architected for performance and agility—designed for organizations that need to handle high-load operations, complex clustering, and elastic scalability with ease.

Innovation at the Heart

Innovation sits at the heart of everything we do. JetHat pioneers AI-driven applications that solve real-world problems and extend the boundaries of what's possible. Our flagship AI products include:

MagAI

An intelligent magazine management platform that streamlines the publication process for journals, research magazines, and editorial content.

Multilingual AI E-Learning

A multilingual AI-based e-learning system that brings interactive, inclusive education to learners across languages and geographies.

OCR Platform

A centralized OCR and editing platform for Indian languages, enabling content creators, researchers, and institutions to digitize and manage regional content efficiently.

Educational Solutions

Language learning apps, e-library solutions, online publishing storefronts, and collaborative multilingual dictionaries that empower users from academic, governmental, and cultural sectors.

Every solution we develop reflects a commitment to localization, accessibility, and long-term impact. We understand the unique challenges faced by multilingual communities and educational institutions, and we respond with technology that speaks their language—literally and figuratively.
